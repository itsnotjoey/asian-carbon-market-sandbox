## 📌 项目简介 | Project Introduction

### 中文版
本项目作为 **Columbia SIPA - Asian Energy Security** 课程的最终成果，构建了一个交互式的地缘政治推演沙盒。通过时间轴可视化，全面展示了从1997年《京都议定书》签署至2060年中国实现碳中和目标的亚洲碳交易演进。它不仅是一个数据分析工具，更是一个理解区域互联、CBAM冲击及东南亚自然碳汇流动等复杂气候政治博弈的窗口。

### English Version
This project, serves as the final project for the **Asian Energy Security** course at **Columbia SIPA**. It is an interactive geopolitical sandbox that visualizes the evolution of carbon markets across Asia—from the signing of the Kyoto Protocol in 1997 to China’s Carbon Neutrality goal in 2060. Beyond a data tool, it offers a window into complex climate politics, including regional ETS interconnections, EU CBAM impacts, and nature-based solution (NbS) credit flows in SE Asia.
